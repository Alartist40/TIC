Welcome!

We are a vibrant, international, and English-speaking community of believers in the heart of Tokyo. Whether you are a long-time resident or just visiting, we welcome you to join us for worship and fellowship.

Worship Services

  • Sabbath School: Saturday, 9:30 AM
  • Divine Worship Service: Saturday, 11:00 AM
  • Prayer Meeting: Wednesday, 7:30 PM (Online)

We also have a Pathfinder club for youth and various small groups that meet throughout the week.
