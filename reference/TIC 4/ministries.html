Our Ministries

At TIC, we believe everyone has a place to serve and grow. Explore our vibrant ministries to find your community and purpose.

Children's Ministry

Engaging, fun, and safe programs designed to teach children about Jesus' love in a way they can understand and enjoy. Every Sabbath morning.

Youth Group

A dynamic community for teens to build friendships, explore their faith, and serve others through meetings, outings, and mission projects.

Prayer Ministry

Join our dedicated team that meets weekly to pray for the needs of our church members, the local community, and the world.

Community Service

Putting our faith into action by organizing outreach programs, food drives, and support for those in need in the Tokyo area.

Women's Fellowship

A supportive network for women of all ages to connect, study the Bible, and encourage one another through life's journeys.

Men's Ministry

Building brotherhood and accountability through regular breakfasts, Bible study, and service opportunities.
