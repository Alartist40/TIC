About TIC

Tokyo International Seventh-day Adventist Church grew from a tiny expat group 20 years ago into a multicultural family of 150+ members representing the Philippines, India, Brazil, USA, Ghana, Kenya, Japan and beyond.

We treasure the Sabbath as a sanctuary in time and seek to share Christ’s love with every English speaker who steps into Tokyo.

Our Core Beliefs

As Seventh-day Adventists we hold the Bible as our only creed. We embrace the 28 fundamental beliefs organised into six categories: God, Humanity, Salvation, The Church, The Christian Life, and Last Day Events.

Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Guenji Imayuki — a second-generation Japanese-Brazilian, former aeronautical engineer, now called to preach the Word. His favourite passage: Isaiah 55:10-11.

Eliane Hosokawa Imayuki — author of nationwide Portuguese textbooks, now passionate about implementing the “centre of influence” model in Tokyo.
