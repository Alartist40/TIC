About Tokyo International Seventh-day Adventist Church

The Tokyo International Seventh-day Adventist Church is a vibrant community dedicated to serving God and the people of Tokyo. Established in 1971, our church has been a beacon of hope and faith for both locals and expatriates.

Our Mission

Our mission is to share the love of Jesus Christ through worship, community service, and outreach programs. We strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone can grow in their faith and experience the joy of fellowship.

Community Involvement

We are actively involved in various community initiatives, including health seminars, youth programs, and humanitarian efforts. Our goal is to make a positive impact in the lives of those around us.

Join Us

We invite you to join us for our weekly services and events. Together, we can grow in faith and make a difference in our community.

Meet Our Pastors

Pastor Guenji Imayuki

Pastor Guenji Imayuki has been serving the church for over a decade. He leads various ministries and programs aimed at fostering a welcoming environment for all.

Background

Originally from Japan, he holds a degree in Theology and has extensive experience in pastoral care and community service.

Pastor Eliane Hosokawa Imayuki

Pastor Eliane Hosokawa Imayuki is dedicated to empowering the youth and women in the church. She is known for her engaging sermons and commitment to discipleship.

Background

With a background in education and counseling, she brings a unique perspective to her ministry, focusing on personal development and spiritual enrichment.
