TIC Content Management Dashboard

Welcome! You can edit content directly in Google Sheets below.

📚 Ministries

Create and edit ministry cards that appear on the website.

Open Ministries Sheet

Required fields: Title, Description

Optional: Icon (emoji or URL)

When you add a row, it automatically appears on the website within 5 minutes.

🕐 Service Schedule

Update weekly service times and locations.

Open Schedule Sheet

Required fields: Program, Time, Location

Time format: "10:00 AM" (use 12-hour format)

Optional: Notes

📅 Events & Calendar

Add upcoming events that appear on the calendar and events list.

Open Events Sheet

Required fields: Title, Date, Time, Description

Date format: YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2026-05-15)

Time format: 2:00 PM (use 12-hour format)

Optional: ImageURL (poster image), RegistrationLink

🎬 Sermons & YouTube

Add sermon videos from YouTube to the sermon library.

Open Sermons Sheet

Required fields: Title, YoutubeID, Speaker, Date

YoutubeID: Just the ID (e.g., dQw4w9WgXcQ), NOT the full URL

How to get YouTube ID: Open video → URL is youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

Optional: Scripture, Duration

ℹ️ Church Information

Update general church info and pastor details.

Open General Info Sheet Open About/Pastor Sheet

General Info: Church name, phone, email, address, YouTube link, announcements

About/Pastor: Mission, Vision, Values, Pastor name, bio, and pastor image URL

💡 Tips & Tricks

  • Website updates: Changes appear within 5 minutes
  • Images: Use full URLs (https://...jpg), not Google Drive links
  • Dates: Always use YYYY-MM-DD format (2026-01-15)
  • Deleting: Delete a row in the sheet to remove it from the website
  • Questions? Contact your developer
